    CURRICULUM VITAE

    WHO IS PROF. DR. MURAT?

    He was born in 1976 in Trabzon. He completed his primary and secondary education at Samsun Ilkadım Primary School and he completed high school education at Samsun 19 Mayıs High School. He started his higher education at Samsun Ondokuz Mayıs University Faculty of Medicine in October 1994. He graduated from this school in July 2000 and received the title of doctor of medicine. In October 2000, Samsun Ondokuz Mayıs University Faculty of Medicine Department of Neurology entered as a specialist student. June 2005 neurology specialist, 14 March 2006 Assistant Professor, 14 March 2012 Associate Professor, in March 2018 became a Professor.

    Since March 14, 2006, he has been continuing his duty as a faculty member in the Department of Neurology of Ondokuz Mayıs University Faculty of Medicine and as the Head of the Department of Neurology since June 2018. He serves on various boards and commissions of our University and Faculty of Medicine. Apart from these duties, he was the Rector advisor of Ondokuz Mayıs University between 2017 and 2020, and a member of the Clinical Research Ethics Committee. Since April 8, 2019, he has continued to work as the Dean of the Faculty of Health Sciences of Ondokuz Mayıs University, since 2016, the head of the Department of Neuroscience at the Institute of Health Sciences of Ondokuz Mayıs University, since 2014, the responsibility of the Clinical Research Unit of the Faculty of Medicine. He is a member of the Turkish Neurological Association, Multiple Sclerosis Working Group of Turkey, Neuroimmunolgy Working Group of Turkey, Neurobehcet Working Group of Turkey, Neurological Environmental Health Working Group of Turkey. He has worked as a primary researcher in more than 40 national and international projects.He has been a consultant in 3 specialty theses in the field of Neurology, 18 Master's theses in the field of Neuroscience, 7 Doctoral theses. He has authored over 100 domestic and international articles, books and book chapters, mainly in the field of multiple sclerosis and neuroimmunology. He has over 200 papers presented at national and international congresses and scientific meetings. He is married and has two children.
